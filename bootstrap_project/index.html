Edu Principles

"We provide guidelines that ensure integrity, critical thinking, and ethical conduct in educational environments. Key principles include academic integrity, which emphasizes honesty and accountability in one's work; research ethics, which focus on the protection and respectful treatment of research participants; and effective communication, which ensures clarity and active listening."
