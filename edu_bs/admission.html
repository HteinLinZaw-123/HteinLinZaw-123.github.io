Admission Criteria

How to Apply

Online Application

Submit your application through a university’s online portal, filling out forms and uploading required documents digitally.

Email Application

Prepare your application materials electronically and send them as attachments to the university’s admissions email address.

Walk-In Application

Physically bring your application materials to the university’s admissions office and submit them in person.

    Admission Criteria for Undergraduate Program:
  • High School Diploma with a minimum GPA of 3.0
  • English Language Proficiency: IELTS score of 6.5
  • One or Two Letters of Recommendation
  • Proof of Financial Support
  • SAT Score of 1200 or ACT Score of 25
    Admission Criteria for Postgraduate Program:
  • Bachelor's Degree: 2:1 or equivalent
  • English Language Proficiency: IELTS score of 6.5
  • Two Letters of Recommendation
  • Proof of Financial Support
  • Work Experience (if required)
